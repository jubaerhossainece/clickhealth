To avail a second opinion from the World Leading Medical Centers (WLMC) you will have to subscribe to an annual membership with
ClickSecondOpinion Service.

As opposed to having a single doctor review the medical file, MediGuide’s hospitals employ the “Consultative Team” approach. This approach joins together a number of different specialists at the WLMC (World Leading Medical Center), each of whom brings separate specializations to the review of a single patient.

Step 1

We will have a family doctor designated for your loved ones who will regularly keep track of the health status and vitals of your parents or loved ones through phone calls and home visits (for additional per visit fee of USD 25) and regularly communicate with you and other care-givers regarding any abnormalities and recommend potential measures.

Step 2

Patient is diagnosed with a condition which is covered under Medical Second Opinion.

Step 3

The member gets in touch with ClickSecondOpinion service center to establish eligibility and then initiates the Medical Second Opinion service through MediGuide International.

Step 4

MediGuide will identify three medical centers available to provide the review and give the names to the patient.

Step 5

Patient and doctor choose the medical center they wish to use for the Medical Second Opinion from the list of 3 provided.

Step 6

Within 10 business days of receipt, the patient will receive a written review from the selected medical center of the original diagnosis and a proposed treatment plan, subject to data protection.
