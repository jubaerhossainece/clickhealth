Designated Family Doctor

Our service employs a synergy between medical monitoring and cutting-edge technology for the prevention of adverse health conditions.

  • We assign a dedicated Family Doctor for your parents who will monitor your parents’ health status through home visits and phone calls
  • A dedicated Medical Assistant will also visit your parents along with the doctor and record their vitals
  • We create a digital medical profile of your parents that you can access through our proprietary app. The profile will be updated regularly
  • Our dedicated Family Doctors will do regular phone follow ups and check up on health status and vitals.
  • Our panel of expert Family Doctors will be always available for your parents anywhere in bangladesh. They can avail on-demand telemedicine service whenever they need.
  • Prompt expert response on any medical query - no matter how big or small the concern is.
  • Regular health updates from your parents from the family doctor, no matter where you are.

Smartphone App and Web Platform

Smartphone app (and web platform) that lets you have access to medical history, tracking of necessary vitals, automated triggers for vitals checking and medicine alerts, and communication with family doctors.

Creation of Digital Health Profile

We will develop a detailed case summary (including medical events, current and past medications) in a digital format that you can use to easily communicate with your doctors, and we will regularly update this case summary.

Digitization of Medical Records

We will digitize the relevant past medical reports so that they are easily accessible and printable whenever needed. You can easily send your entire medical history with the click of a single button to your doctors in country and also abroad.

Tele-Consultation On-Demand

Tele-Consultation with a wide range of specialists based in Bangladesh
